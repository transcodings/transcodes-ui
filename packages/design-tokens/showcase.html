Ink System

Text Hierarchy

Primary Heading (ink-black)

Used for main titles and most important content. Maximum contrast and visual weight.

Secondary Heading (ink-dark)

Used for section titles and secondary emphasis. Strong but not dominant.

Body text uses ink-medium for comfortable reading on extended content. This color provides good contrast while being easy on the eyes for longer reading sessions.

Metadata and timestamps use ink-light for supplementary information that shouldn't compete with main content.

Disabled or placeholder text uses ink-faint to indicate non-interactive or unavailable content.
